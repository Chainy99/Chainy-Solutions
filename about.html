Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some common questions about partnering, working, and building with Innovoo Solutions.

How can I partner with Innovoo Solutions to sell my software products?

Simply reach out to us through our 'Join Us' page. We work with partners to resell, customize, and deliver your software solutions to new markets, backed by our sales and technical teams.

Do you provide custom development for startups and businesses?

Yes. Whether you are launching a new product or transforming an existing system, we offer tailor-made software, mobile apps, AI integrations, and cloud deployments to meet your business goals.

Can freelancers and developers collaborate with Innovoo?

Absolutely. We welcome talented developers, designers, and freelancers to join our extended team. You can collaborate on exciting projects under Innovoo’s client umbrella while working flexibly.

Is there a fixed pricing for your services?

Our services are fully customized based on your specific needs. After understanding your requirements, we prepare a detailed quotation and flexible engagement model for you — no hidden fees.

What industries do you serve?

We work across multiple industries including Healthcare, Education, Finance, Logistics, Retail, and more. Our scalable technology solutions adapt to various business models and market needs.
